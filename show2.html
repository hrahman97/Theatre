Prodigy

Weaving together shades of Karl Marx and the subprime mortgage crisis, Prodigy creates an incredible tapestry of post-Reconstruction tensions but still with engaging, grounded scene work. Like William Howard Taft meets Drama, Prodigy's all-in brand of drama is sure to leave you in disbelief and at the edge of your seat.
